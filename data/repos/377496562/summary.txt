AppFlowy is an open-source collaborative workspace application built with Flutter and Rust that serves as an alternative to Notion for productivity and knowledge management. The cross-platform application targets teams, content creators, and organizations requiring note-taking, project management, documentation, and wiki functionality while maintaining data privacy and control. It implements features including Kanban boards, databases, AI integration, and real-time collaboration across desktop (macOS, Windows, Linux) and mobile platforms (iOS, Android). The codebase leverages Flutter for cross-platform UI development and Rust for backend services, addressing the content management and team collaboration domain with self-hosting capabilities and extensible architecture for customization.
