The `cloudinary_gem` is a Ruby on Rails SDK facilitating integration with the Cloudinary media API. It enables developers to transform, optimize, and manage images and videos within Ruby on Rails applications, including asset upload, generation of image and video tags, and integration with CarrierWave and Active Storage. The SDK provides functionalities for asset optimization, transformation, and secure URL generation, leveraging the Cloudinary platform for media delivery and management. This library, designed for Ruby developers integrating Cloudinary's media services into their Rails projects, is a Ruby gem.
