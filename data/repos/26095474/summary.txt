`plan9port` provides a port of Plan 9 libraries and programs to Unix-like operating systems. This project enables users to utilize Plan 9's functionalities on various Unix systems, offering access to its tools and potentially incorporating its design philosophies. Written in C, the project facilitates installation via a `mk` based build system, mirroring aspects of the Plan 9 environment on different platforms. It functions as a port of software, thus, it allows users to experience Plan 9 features on Unix environments.
