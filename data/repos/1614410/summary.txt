FFmpeg is a comprehensive multimedia processing library and command-line tool for manipulating, converting, and streaming audio and video content. Primarily written in C, it provides a wide range of functionalities through libraries such as `libavcodec` for codecs, `libavformat` for container formats and protocols, and `libavfilter` for audio/video processing. This collection includes the `ffmpeg` command-line utility, `ffplay` for playback, and `ffprobe` for media analysis, serving as a core component for multimedia encoding, decoding, and streaming applications; it is targeted towards developers working with multimedia processing.
