The `kamranahmedse/developer-roadmap` repository provides interactive roadmaps, guides, and educational content designed to aid developers in career growth. It offers structured learning paths and resources across various domains, including frontend, backend, DevOps, and specific technologies like Angular, React, Node.js, and Go. Built with TypeScript, this project serves as an educational resource, presenting roadmap visualizations and related materials to enhance developer skills and knowledge acquisition within the software development ecosystem. The project's focus is on providing guidance and curated content for developers at all career stages, facilitating skill development through structured learning paths and technology-specific roadmaps.
