`home-assistant/core` is an open-source home automation platform designed for local control and privacy, leveraging the Internet of Things (IoT) domain. It enables users to manage and automate their home devices through a centralized interface. Built primarily with Python and utilizing `asyncio` for asynchronous operations, the project supports integration with numerous devices and protocols, including MQTT. This standalone application facilitates user interaction with a wide range of connected devices, creating a unified smart home experience.
