This repository provides a PyTorch implementation for restoring old photographs, addressing image degradation and imperfections. It leverages Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and deep latent space translation techniques for image manipulation and restoration tasks, including scratch removal and face enhancement. The project offers functionalities through CLI and GUI interfaces, including scratch detection, global restoration, and a face enhancement pipeline. It is a research artifact and example project focused on old photo restoration, utilizing Python with dependencies including PyTorch. The target audience is likely researchers and practitioners in computer vision and image processing.
