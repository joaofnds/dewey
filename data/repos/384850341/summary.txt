`fzf-lua` is a Neovim plugin enhancing developer productivity by providing fuzzy finding capabilities within the editor. Written in Lua, it offers a modern implementation of `fzf.vim`, integrating with the `fzf` command-line fuzzy finder for interactive selection of files, buffers, and other Neovim entities.  The plugin leverages Lua's extension capabilities to offer extensive customization options, including user-defined keymaps, actions, and flexible configuration of the fzf interface itself, with integrations for previewing content from multiple sources. This plugin is designed for Neovim users seeking an advanced fuzzy finding experience, extending and replacing the functionality of similar plugins.
