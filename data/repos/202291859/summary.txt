Chatwoot is an open-source, self-hosted customer support platform designed as an alternative to solutions like Intercom and Zendesk. Built with Ruby on Rails, Vue.js, and leveraging technologies like ActionCable, Docker, and Sass, it provides an omni-channel support desk with live chat, email support, and integrations with platforms like WhatsApp and Facebook. Key features include a dashboard for managing conversations, help center portal capabilities, and collaboration tools such as internal notes, labels, and canned responses. This is a standalone web application intended for businesses seeking to improve their customer support workflows.
