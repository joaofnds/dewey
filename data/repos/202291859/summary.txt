Chatwoot is an open-source omnichannel customer support platform built with Ruby on Rails backend and Vue.js frontend, targeting businesses seeking alternatives to proprietary helpdesk solutions like Intercom and Zendesk. The application provides a unified inbox for managing customer conversations across multiple channels (live chat, email, WhatsApp, social media) with features including contact management, automated workflows, team collaboration tools, and AI-powered response automation. Built for self-hosting with Docker deployment support, it serves customer support teams, system administrators, and businesses requiring full control over their customer communication data while maintaining scalability through ActionCable for real-time messaging and comprehensive API integrations for extending functionality across existing business workflows.
