Kong is a cloud-native API gateway and AI gateway, designed for managing and securing microservices and APIs. Built primarily in Lua, it offers advanced routing, load balancing, health checks, authentication, and authorization functionalities. Kong supports plugins for extending core features, including those for AI traffic management with multi-LLM implementations and Kubernetes ingress controller support. It is a standalone application, intended for DevOps engineers and developers who need to control and secure their API traffic.
