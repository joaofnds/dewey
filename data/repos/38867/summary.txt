`rails-i18n` is a Ruby gem providing locale data and related utilities for internationalization (I18n) in Ruby on Rails applications. It centralizes locale data collection, including translation files and pluralization rules for numerous languages, directly enhancing Rails' I18n capabilities. The gem supports various Rails versions, offers modular configuration for specific I18n components, and facilitates manual installation for locale files. It functions as a library, primarily targeting Ruby on Rails developers seeking to implement multilingual support within their applications.
