Microsoft Activation Scripts (MAS) is an open-source, command-line utility for activating Microsoft Windows and Office products. It provides multiple activation methods, including HWID, Ohook, TSforge, KMS38, and Online KMS, for bypassing licensing restrictions. MAS is implemented as a batch script (Batchfile) and offers advanced troubleshooting capabilities to assist users experiencing activation issues. The project is a standalone, command-line application primarily targeted at users seeking to activate their Microsoft software.
