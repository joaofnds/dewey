`lerrua/remote-jobs-brazil` is an "awesome-list" style curated directory of Brazilian and worldwide companies known to offer remote work opportunities, primarily within the tech sector. This project functions as a categorized, alphabetically-ordered list maintained via pull requests, providing company names and website links. The repository is designed as a community resource, allowing users to discover remote job opportunities and contribute to the list's accuracy and completeness. This is a data aggregation project, acting as a reference for job seekers.
