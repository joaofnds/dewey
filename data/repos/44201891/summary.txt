This repository serves as a curated directory of Brazilian companies offering remote work opportunities, primarily targeting the technology sector. It functions as an "awesome list" resource containing a structured table of company names and their corresponding career/hiring websites, organized alphabetically for easy navigation. The repository addresses the specific need for remote job seekers in Brazil's tech industry to discover companies with remote-friendly policies across various domains including software development, fintech, e-commerce, healthcare tech, and digital services. The target users are primarily software developers, engineers, and other tech professionals seeking remote employment opportunities within Brazilian companies or Brazil-based operations of international firms. The repository is maintained through community contributions via pull requests and includes companies ranging from startups to established enterprises like Amazon, Accenture, and major Brazilian tech firms.
