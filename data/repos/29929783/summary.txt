Mezzano is a standalone operating system entirely written in Common Lisp, providing a complete environment for running applications. Key features include a USB stack, improved filesystem support (EXT2/3/4, FAT32), SMP and multicore support, a generational garbage collector, and hardware accelerated 3D support. It includes a CLOS implementation, a new SSA-based compiler backend, and networking capabilities, targeting users seeking a Lisp-based operating system with a focus on conformance, stability, and performance.
