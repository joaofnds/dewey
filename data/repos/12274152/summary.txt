`lightline.vim` is a Vim plugin providing a highly configurable and lightweight statusline and tabline. It allows users to customize the display of information, including mode, filename, and integration with other plugins like `vim-fugitive`, by defining components and component functions in Vim script.  The plugin offers a minimal core with extensive configurability, supporting multiple colorschemes and various installation methods (Vim packages, Pathogen, Vundle, NeoBundle, and vim-plug). Its design emphasizes orthogonality, enabling users to integrate with other plugins without relying on their internal implementations. Primarily, this is a developer productivity plugin designed for Vim users seeking to enhance their editing experience through a customizable statusline.
