`ossu/computer-science` is a curated, open-source curriculum for self-taught computer science education. The repository provides a comprehensive path through computer science fundamentals, mirroring undergraduate degree requirements with courses sourced from universities like MIT, Harvard, and Princeton. This project offers a structured learning experience, organizing courses into introductory, core, advanced, and final project phases, and utilizes HTML for a course catalog and community links. It serves as an educational resource targeting individuals seeking a free, self-directed, and well-rounded computer science education, enabling them to learn core concepts and build practical skills through online materials and community support.
