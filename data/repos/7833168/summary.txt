Kibana is a browser-based analytics and search dashboard designed for the Elastic Stack, providing data visualization and exploration capabilities for Elasticsearch. This TypeScript-based application facilitates the creation of dashboards, metrics monitoring, and other visualizations, enabling users to gain insights from their Elasticsearch data. As a standalone web application, Kibana allows users to search, analyze, and visualize data through its interactive interface, serving as a primary component within the Elastic observability ecosystem. It is intended for users who need to explore and analyze large datasets stored in Elasticsearch.
