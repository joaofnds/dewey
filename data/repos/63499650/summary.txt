`awesome-hyper` is a curated list repository serving as a directory of plugins, themes, and resources for the Hyper terminal emulator. It functions as a community-driven, categorized collection of extensions and customizations, covering productivity enhancements, theming options, development tools, and fun utilities designed to modify and extend Hyper's functionality. The project type is an "awesome list," which lists links to various npm packages and resources, primarily targeting Hyper terminal users and developers interested in improving their terminal experience.
