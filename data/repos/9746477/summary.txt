Sonic Pi is a live-coding environment and musical instrument written in C++, designed for music composition and education. It allows users to create and manipulate sounds in real-time through code, functioning as both a live coding instrument for professional artists and a learning tool for schools. Core features include synthesis capabilities, facilitating musical expression and exploring concepts related to liveness, time, and concurrency. This standalone application targets musicians, educators, and anyone interested in exploring the intersection of art, technology, and education through music creation.
