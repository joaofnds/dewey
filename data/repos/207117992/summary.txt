`viraptor/reverse-interview` is a curated list of questions designed to assist tech job applicants in preparing for and conducting job interviews. The repository primarily functions as a developer productivity resource, providing a comprehensive collection of questions categorized by topic, including the role, tech stack, team dynamics, company culture, compensation, and remote work policies. It features translations into numerous languages, offering a valuable resource for a global audience. This project is a documentation-driven collection, serving as a reference guide and a starting point for candidates seeking to assess potential employers.
