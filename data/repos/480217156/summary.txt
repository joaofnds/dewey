GreptimeDB is an open-source, cloud-native, and distributed time-series database designed for unified observability, handling metrics, logs, and traces. Built in Rust, it supports SQL, PromQL, and streaming queries, enabling sub-second querying at PB scale. Its cloud-native architecture integrates with object storage and Kubernetes, offering high performance through a distributed query engine and optimized columnar storage. It's a standalone database application targeting developers and operations teams needing a performant and scalable solution for analyzing observability data.
