`urchin` is a 34-key wireless keyboard design utilizing the ZMK firmware and supporting nice!view displays and hotswap sockets. This project, a keyboard hardware design, provides KiCad files for a custom PCB, along with 3D-printable case designs and switch plate options. It features native support for nice!view, a Ferris/Sweep layout, and is designed for wireless operation with only Choc v1 switches and hotswap sockets. It is intended for keyboard enthusiasts and DIY builders interested in constructing split ergonomic keyboards.
