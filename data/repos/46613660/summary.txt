Photopea is a web-based image editor designed for raster and vector graphics editing. It provides support for opening, editing, and exporting a wide range of image file formats, including PSD, AI, Sketch, PNG, JPG, GIF, and various raw camera formats.  Functionality includes complex format support and animated GIF creation/editing.  This repository primarily serves as a platform for bug reports, feature requests, and discussions related to the core Photopea application, which operates as a standalone online tool.
