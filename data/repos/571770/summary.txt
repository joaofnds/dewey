Pandoc is a universal markup converter, implemented as a Haskell library and command-line tool. It facilitates conversions between numerous markup formats, including Markdown, HTML, LaTeX, DOCX, and many others, enabling document interchange and publishing workflows. Key functionalities include parsing various input formats into an abstract syntax tree and writing to diverse output formats, with support for custom filters to modify the intermediate representation. As a standalone application and a library, it's geared towards developers and content creators needing to convert and process documents in various formats, offering a robust solution for document conversion and publishing tasks.
