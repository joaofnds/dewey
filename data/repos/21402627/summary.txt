`awesome-elixir` is a curated list, essentially a directory, of Elixir and Erlang libraries, resources, and related tools. It provides categorized listings spanning various domains, including actors, authentication, testing, and web development, as well as resources like books and podcasts for Elixir developers. The project functions as a comprehensive reference for the Elixir ecosystem, acting as a resource for developers looking to find tools and learn about the language and its applications. It is a community-driven resource designed for Elixir programmers of all levels.
