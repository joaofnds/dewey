Pedestal is a Clojure library designed for server-side development, offering a framework to build web applications and services. It provides features including asynchronous request handling, Server Sent Events (SSE) and WebSockets support, integrated logging, metrics, and tracing, and integrates with Jetty 12 and Http-Kit. This library emphasizes principles of simplicity, power, and focus, catering to Clojure developers seeking a robust and extensible backend framework.
