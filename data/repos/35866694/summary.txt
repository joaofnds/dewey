Rocket.Chat is a real-time communication platform designed for secure, open-source team collaboration and customer engagement. Built with TypeScript and utilizing JavaScript and Meteor, it provides features for internal and cross-company conversations, omnichannel customer service, and custom messaging integration within applications. This standalone, web-based application offers a marketplace for integrations and targets organizations requiring robust data protection, positioning itself as an alternative to proprietary chat solutions. The project is a self-hosted or cloud-hosted application with clients for web and mobile platforms, providing a full communications ecosystem for business use cases.
