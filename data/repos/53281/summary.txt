`common-lisp-stat` is a Common Lisp library providing statistical computing functionality, mirroring the capabilities of R and building upon the foundations of LispStat. This library offers statistical analysis tools and methods for use within the Common Lisp programming environment. It aims to provide a robust statistical computation platform, designed for researchers and practitioners within the scientific and data analysis domains. It functions as a library, extending Common Lisp with domain-specific statistical operations and techniques.
