EAF is a Python-based framework designed to significantly enhance Emacs' graphical capabilities by integrating modern applications. It provides a range of functionalities including a web browser, PDF viewer, music player, file manager, and more, all accessible within the Emacs environment. Built upon Python, Qt6, and potentially JavaScript, EAF aims to extend Emacs with a modern GUI while preserving its extensibility and customizability. This is a framework, primarily targeting Emacs users seeking to expand their editor's capabilities with integrated graphical applications and enhanced performance.
