`mu` is a standalone C++ application focused on indexing and searching maildirs, integrated with an Emacs mail client and Guile bindings. It provides functionalities for efficiently managing and searching email stored in the Maildir format. The project's architecture facilitates both command-line interaction and Emacs integration, enabling users to search and access emails directly from within the editor, with added extensibility via its Guile bindings. The primary audience likely consists of users who heavily rely on Emacs for email management and desire a fast, efficient mail indexing and searching solution.
