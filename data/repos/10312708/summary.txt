`dpl` is a Ruby-based command-line tool designed for continuous deployment to various service providers, specifically integrating with CI/CD pipelines like Travis CI. Its core functionality centers on deploying code, artifacts, or packages to a wide range of platforms, including cloud providers and package repositories.  The tool supports a modular architecture with numerous providers, each offering a specific set of deployment options and configurations, demonstrating its flexibility across deployment targets.  It is a standalone application for developers and DevOps engineers to automate their deployment processes.
