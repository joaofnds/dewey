Unleash is an open-source feature management platform built in TypeScript, providing a comprehensive solution for feature flagging and toggling in software development. It allows developers to control feature releases, experiment with new functionalities, and manage deployments through activation strategies and variants. The platform offers server and client SDKs for various languages, enabling integration into diverse application stacks. Unleash provides a web-based application and supporting components as a self-managed or hosted service, targeting software engineers and platform teams focused on continuous delivery and DevOps practices.
