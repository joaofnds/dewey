Mailspring is a cross-platform, open-source email client for Mac, Windows, and Linux, designed as a successor to Nylas Mail. Built with Electron, TypeScript, and React, it features a C++ sync engine based on Mailcore2 for improved performance and reduced resource usage. Key functionalities include a unified inbox, snooze, send later, mail rules, and templates, with additional features available via a subscription. This is a standalone, GUI application targeting end-users seeking a modern and performant email client.
