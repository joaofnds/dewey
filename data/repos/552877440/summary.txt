Hyperswitch is an open-source payments orchestration platform, engineered in Rust, designed to facilitate fast, reliable, and cost-effective payment processing. It provides a single API for accessing various payment ecosystems, supporting authorization, authentication, and post-payment workflows, including refunds and chargeback handling. Key components include a backend for payment processing, an SDK for frontend integration, and a control center for managing the payments stack. Hyperswitch offers customizable routing, intelligent retry mechanisms, and integrates with PostgreSQL and Redis, targeting digital businesses seeking a modular and transparent payments infrastructure.
