The **awesome-cerebro** repository is a curated collection of plugins and resources for Cerebro, a cross-platform application launcher and productivity tool. This resource catalog targets developers, system administrators, and power users who utilize Cerebro to enhance their workflow efficiency through extensible plugins. The repository organizes NPM-published plugins across categories including development tools (cerebro-npm, cerebro-github, cerebro-devdocs), system utilities (cerebro-shell, cerebro-kill, cerebro-ip), web services integration (cerebro-stackoverflow, cerebro-weather, cerebro-imdb), and text manipulation tools (cerebro-hash, cerebro-base64). The collection serves the desktop productivity and automation domain, providing a centralized discovery mechanism for JavaScript-based Cerebro extensions that integrate with various APIs, development platforms, and system-level operations to streamline command-line style interactions within a GUI launcher interface.
