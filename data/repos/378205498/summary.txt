Kalendar is a Kirigami-based calendar and task management application for the Plasma desktop and mobile environments, leveraging the Akonadi framework for synchronization with external services like Nextcloud, GMail, and Exchange. It allows users to add, edit, and delete events and tasks, synchronizing changes across devices. Built with C++, it utilizes KDE Frameworks and Qt, with dependencies including KDEPIM-related libraries for calendar and contact support. It is a standalone GUI application designed for end-users to manage their calendars and tasks, offering month, week, and schedule views.
