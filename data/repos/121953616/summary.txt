`go-internals` is a book providing in-depth coverage of the internal workings of the Go programming language, targeting experienced Go developers. The project explores Go's internals, including assembly, interfaces, and the garbage collector, utilizing code, diagrams, and experiments to validate concepts. This resource aims to provide advanced knowledge beyond introductory materials, offering a deeper understanding of Go's implementation. It is a book project written in Go, designed to remain current with evolving Go versions and encouraging community contributions.
