Pigsty is a comprehensive, self-hosting PostgreSQL distribution designed as a free alternative to cloud-based RDS. Primarily written in Shell and leveraging Ansible for automation, it offers a battery-included environment for PostgreSQL, featuring built-in high availability, point-in-time recovery, and comprehensive monitoring based on Prometheus and Grafana. Key functionalities include simplified installation, automated configuration, a wide selection of PostgreSQL extensions, and support for various PostgreSQL kernel forks, including Babelfish for MSSQL compatibility. As a standalone application and toolbox, Pigsty targets system administrators and DevOps engineers seeking to deploy and manage PostgreSQL clusters with ease and control.
