`openpilot` is a Python-based operating system designed to upgrade driver-assistance systems in over 300 supported vehicles. It provides advanced driver-assistance features and leverages robotics principles to control vehicle functions. The project offers a modular architecture with features including sensor data logging, safety testing, and supports over-the-air updates. It is a standalone application that requires a comma 3/3X device and targets users looking to enhance their car's driver assistance capabilities.
