`lux` is a fast, Go-based command-line tool and library for downloading videos from various online platforms, including YouTube, Bilibili, and others listed in the "Supported Sites" section. It provides functionalities such as playlist downloads, resuming interrupted downloads, cookie support for authenticated access, proxy configuration, multi-threading for faster downloads, and options for output path and filename customization. The tool leverages FFmpeg for post-processing video files and supports various installation methods, including `go install`, Homebrew, and package managers for different operating systems. This standalone CLI application is designed for users seeking a simple yet powerful video downloader.
