Spotube is a cross-platform, open-source music player application built with Flutter, targeting desktop (Linux, macOS, Windows) and Android platforms. It provides ad-free music playback by leveraging public music metadata providers and YouTube, Piped.video, or JioSaavn as audio sources, offering features like freely downloadable tracks, time-synced lyrics, and anonymous login.  The application utilizes Dart as its primary programming language. It is a standalone application focused on providing a user-friendly music listening experience.
