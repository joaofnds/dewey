`emacs-slack` is an Emacs Lisp package providing a Slack client integrated directly within the Emacs text editor. It enables users to interact with Slack workspaces, including sending and receiving messages, managing channels and direct messages, and utilizing features like reactions, emoji, and file uploads. The project leverages Emacs Lisp, with dependencies including `alert`, `circe`, `emojify` (optional), `oauth2`, `request`, and `websocket`, and facilitates integration with other Emacs packages such as `helm-slack` and `ol-emacs-slack`. This plugin primarily targets Emacs users seeking enhanced productivity through Slack access directly from their editor.
