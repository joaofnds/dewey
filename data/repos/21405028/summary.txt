`gotraining` is a training material repository focused on teaching the Go programming language. It provides comprehensive course materials, including videos, a companion book, and practical exercises, designed to accelerate Go developers' knowledge and productivity. The repository covers language mechanics, design principles, and production software practices, with an emphasis on performance considerations. It is a collection of examples, and reference materials for developers seeking to learn Go, and is accompanied by supplemental content from the trainers, including recordings, articles, and related resources.
