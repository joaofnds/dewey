`invisible_captcha` is a Ruby library providing unobtrusive and flexible spam protection for Rails applications. It utilizes honeypot, time-sensitive submission, and IP-based spinner validation techniques to detect and mitigate spam, offering a better user experience by avoiding traditional CAPTCHAs. The library integrates with Rails forms, controllers, and views via helpers and before_actions, allowing customization through options like custom honeypot names, spam callbacks, and I18n support, along with instrumentation for advanced logging. It is a Ruby gem designed for Rails developers seeking to improve the security of their web applications by preventing spambot submissions.
