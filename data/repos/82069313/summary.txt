This repository provides a collection of PNG artwork featuring the Go gopher, derived from the original designs by Renee French. The primary function is to offer print-ready artwork files suitable for derivative works and promotional materials, particularly stickers. The project's assets are released under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License, restricting commercial use. It is a collection of image assets, designed for visual use and community engagement within the Go programming ecosystem.
