Microsoft Presidio is a Python-based data privacy and protection SDK designed for enterprise data governance and compliance workflows. It provides automated detection, redaction, masking, and anonymization of personally identifiable information (PII) and protected health information (PHI) across text, images, and structured datasets. The framework leverages natural language processing with spaCy and transformers, named entity recognition, regex pattern matching, and machine learning models to identify sensitive data like SSNs, credit cards, names, and locations. Targeted at data engineers, privacy officers, and compliance teams in healthcare, finance, and regulated industries, Presidio offers customizable recognition pipelines, multi-language support, and deployment options including Docker and Kubernetes for production data processing environments.
