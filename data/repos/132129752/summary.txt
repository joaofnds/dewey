`microsoft/presidio` is a Python-based, open-source framework designed for detecting, redacting, and anonymizing sensitive data, including Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Protected Health Information (PHI), across various data formats. It employs Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, pattern matching, and customizable processing pipelines to identify and obfuscate sensitive elements within text, images, and structured data. Key functionalities include data masking and redaction capabilities, supporting de-identification and data privacy compliance efforts. This library targets data scientists, software engineers, and security professionals focused on data privacy and compliance.
