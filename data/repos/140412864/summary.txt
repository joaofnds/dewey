`Evilginx2` is a standalone application written in Go designed for man-in-the-middle phishing attacks, enabling the capture of login credentials and session cookies to bypass two-factor authentication. It implements its own HTTP and DNS servers to act as a proxy, intercepting traffic between a browser and the targeted website. This framework facilitates reverse proxy phishing, allowing attackers to steal user credentials by mimicking legitimate login pages. It is a standalone application aimed at penetration testers and security professionals for educational and assessment purposes.
