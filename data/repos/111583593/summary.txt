scrcpy is a standalone application written in C that mirrors and controls Android devices connected via USB or TCP/IP. Its core functionality includes real-time screen mirroring, audio forwarding (Android 11+), and control via a computer's keyboard and mouse without requiring root access or device-side app installation. Utilizing libraries like FFmpeg and SDL2, scrcpy offers features such as recording, virtual display, and configurable video quality, catering to users who require a lightweight, high-performance solution for interacting with their Android devices on their computers. This developer productivity tool allows control of Android devices on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
