`basarat/typescript-book` is a free and open-source, comprehensive guide to TypeScript. It serves as a learning resource, providing detailed explanations and examples for TypeScript developers. The book leverages TypeScript and focuses on practical application, drawing on insights from the TypeScript community and Stack Overflow. Its primary function is to educate users on professional TypeScript development, featuring a focus on common issues encountered by developers. It is available online and in various downloadable formats, with active community translations.
