`oci.dag.dev` is a Go-based web server designed for exploring the contents of an OCI registry, specifically targeting container image metadata. Its core functionality involves inspecting and presenting data from OCI images, facilitating the examination of their components and structure. The project utilizes Go and leverages tooling like `ko` for building and deploying on Cloud Run. It's a standalone application intended for developers involved in container image management and related infrastructure, emphasizing performance and potentially prioritizing specific use cases within a Google Cloud Platform environment.
