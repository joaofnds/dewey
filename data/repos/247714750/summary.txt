ZITADEL is a Go-based identity infrastructure platform providing authentication, authorization, and user management functionalities, targeting secure access and multi-tenancy needs. Key features include support for OpenID Connect, OAuth2, SAML, multi-factor authentication (2FA), passkeys (FIDO2), and SCIM 2.0. This standalone application offers a comprehensive solution for identity and access management, including self-service capabilities, with a focus on both B2B and CIAM (Customer Identity and Access Management) scenarios, providing a turnkey solution for authentication and authorization needs.
