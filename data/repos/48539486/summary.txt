`crux` is an Emacs Lisp library providing a collection of interactive commands and extensions to enhance the Emacs editing experience. It offers functionalities like file operations (e.g., open with external program, rename), text manipulation (e.g., smart kill line, indent), and buffer management (e.g., kill other buffers, duplicate line), along with utility commands. The library includes several advised functions and minor modes, notably `crux-reopen-as-root-mode`, to further extend Emacs's capabilities. Primarily, `crux` is a package/plugin designed to improve Emacs users' productivity by offering a suite of useful editing and buffer management features.
