`io12/org-fragtog` is an Emacs Lisp plugin designed to automatically toggle LaTeX fragment previews within Org mode documents. Its core functionality involves dynamically showing or hiding LaTeX previews as the text cursor moves into or out of their respective regions, enhancing the user experience for document creation and editing. This plugin leverages Emacs Lisp and integrates directly within the Org mode environment, targeting users who author documents that heavily incorporate LaTeX formulas and content previews. This is a developer productivity plugin/extension.
