This Emacs Lisp project acts as an extension to enhance the visual representation of icons within Ivy and Counsel interfaces. Functionality centers on displaying icons, likely representing files, modes, or other Emacs elements within the completion buffers. It is a plugin/extension designed to improve the user experience for Emacs users utilizing the Ivy and Counsel packages for navigation and selection.
