Rancher is a complete container management platform, written in Go, designed for orchestrating and managing containerized applications, particularly those running on Kubernetes. It provides a user-friendly interface and streamlined workflows for deploying, managing, and scaling containerized workloads across diverse infrastructure environments. Key features include container orchestration, Docker support, Kubernetes integration, and centralized management capabilities. This is a standalone, open-source application targeting DevOps teams and system administrators seeking to simplify and automate container deployment and management in production.
