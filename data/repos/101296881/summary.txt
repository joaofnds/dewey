`mtdvio/every-programmer-should-know` is a curated, educational collection of resources aimed at software developers, encompassing diverse technical topics relevant to all skill levels. The repository functions as a categorized list, linking to external materials such as articles, videos, and books covering subjects including algorithms, data structures, distributed systems, security, UX/Usability, and career development. It serves as a reference and learning guide for programmers seeking to broaden their knowledge base and improve their skillset. This is a collection of links, categorized by subject matter.
