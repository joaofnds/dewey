The `Cookbook` repository is a comprehensive, Python-based resource designed to educate and guide individuals in the field of data engineering.  It acts as a digital book, offering a wide range of content including introductory material, best practices, case studies, interview questions, and curated lists of data sources, books, courses, and podcasts related to data engineering. The repository functions as a knowledge repository and educational tool, covering topics such as big data technologies, cloud platforms, and advanced engineering skills, with sections dedicated to specific tools and platforms within the data engineering ecosystem. This project type is a learning guide and code example project, targeting data engineers and those seeking to expand their data engineering skill set.
