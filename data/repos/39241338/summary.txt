`Schemer2` is a Go-based command-line application designed for generating and converting terminal colorschemes. It enables users to read color configurations from various terminal formats and images, then output them in a variety of formats including images, text, and configurations for numerous terminal emulators. Key features include image-based color extraction, customizable color difference thresholds and brightness controls, and support for formats such as Xresources, Konsole, iTerm2, and more. This is a standalone, developer productivity tool aimed at users customizing their terminal appearance.
