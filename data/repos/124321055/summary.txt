Porcupine is a highly accurate and lightweight on-device wake word engine, enabling always-listening voice-activated applications. Built using deep learning, it's optimized for low-resource environments and offers cross-platform support, including Python, .NET, Java, Unity, Flutter, React Native, Android, iOS, Web (WebAssembly), NodeJS, Rust, C, and microcontrollers. The project provides SDKs and demo applications across various platforms, enabling developers to integrate custom wake word detection functionality into their projects. This library targets developers seeking to build voice-enabled applications with a focus on efficiency and portability.
