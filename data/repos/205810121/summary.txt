CloverBootloader is a C-based bootloader designed for macOS, Windows, and Linux operating systems, functioning in both UEFI and legacy BIOS modes. It enables booting from UEFI firmware or through CloverEFI UEFI firmware emulation, offering a customizable GUI with themes, resolution controls, and multilingual help. Key features include the ability to save ACPI tables, graphics firmware, and screenshots, alongside NVRAM manipulation, and CD/DVD ejection functionalities. This is a standalone application, primarily targeted toward users seeking to boot multiple operating systems or customize the boot process on their hardware.
