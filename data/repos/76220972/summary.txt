This repository serves as a comprehensive resource for DIY mechanical keyboard enthusiasts, providing a curated list of keyboard layouts, components, firmware, tools, and tutorials. It catalogs a diverse range of mechanical keyboard designs, including normal, ergonomic, ortholinear, and macropad layouts, with links to related projects and resources, including PCBs, cases, and keycap information. The project functions as a directory and educational guide, showcasing various keyboard projects and components, with a primary focus on the custom keyboard community. It targets individuals interested in building or modifying mechanical keyboards.
