Super Productivity is a TypeScript-based Electron application that serves as an advanced task management and time tracking solution for productivity-focused professionals. Built for cross-platform deployment (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS), it integrates with developer and project management platforms including Jira, GitHub, GitLab, and OpenProject to synchronize tasks and issues. The application implements timeboxing methodologies, automated time tracking, and calendar integration to support agile workflows and project management practices. Target users include software developers, project managers, and knowledge workers who need comprehensive task organization with external tool integration. The codebase leverages Angular framework within Electron for desktop applications and Ionic/Capacitor for mobile deployment, addressing the need for unified productivity management across development and project management ecosystems.
