`igrigorik/videospeed` is a JavaScript-based Google Chrome extension designed to control the playback speed of HTML5 videos. It enables users to easily accelerate, decelerate, rewind, and advance videos, addressing the discrepancy between reading and speech rates to improve engagement. Key features include keyboard shortcuts for speed adjustments, a visual speed indicator, and customizable settings. This plugin is specifically designed as a Chrome extension, targeting users who consume video content online.
