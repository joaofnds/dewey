`Bull` is a high-performance, Redis-based job and message queue library for Node.js, designed for handling distributed tasks with features including job prioritization, scheduling, rate limiting, and retries. As a JavaScript library, it provides core functionalities for managing asynchronous job processing, enabling the creation of robust and scalable applications. It is used by organizations such as Atlassian and Mozilla. Its architecture is centered around a Redis backend for persistent job storage and utilizes a polling-free design for minimal CPU usage.
