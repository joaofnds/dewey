Logseq is an open-source, privacy-focused, desktop and mobile platform for knowledge management and collaboration, built with Clojure and ClojureScript. It facilitates note-taking, knowledge graph construction, and information organization, supporting Markdown and Org-mode formats. Key functionalities include a graph-based note-linking system, whiteboard capabilities for visual knowledge representation, and extensibility through a plugin API. This standalone application targets individuals and teams seeking improved productivity through a structured approach to managing and connecting knowledge.
