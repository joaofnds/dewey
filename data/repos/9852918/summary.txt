Ghost is an open-source headless CMS built on Node.js and JavaScript, specifically designed for modern digital publishing and content management. It serves the web development and digital publishing domains by providing a professional blogging platform with integrated membership management, subscription handling, and newsletter functionality. The system offers both self-hosted deployment options via CLI tools and managed hosting services, featuring automatic SSL setup, CDN integration, and RESTful APIs for headless implementations. Ghost targets web developers, content creators, journalists, and publishers who need a scalable, customizable publishing platform that can integrate with existing development workflows while supporting modern web technologies and content monetization strategies.
