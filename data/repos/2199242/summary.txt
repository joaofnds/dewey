`evil-surround` is an Emacs Lisp extension providing functionality analogous to the `surround.vim` plugin for the Evil mode vi-emulation layer. It allows users to quickly surround text with paired delimiters, such as parentheses, brackets, or quotes. This plugin enhances text editing efficiency within the Emacs environment, specifically targeting users of Evil mode. As such, it is a plugin designed to extend the functionality of the Evil editing mode within Emacs.
