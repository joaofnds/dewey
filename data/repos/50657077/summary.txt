Gerbil is a dialect of Scheme designed for systems programming, built on the Gambit runtime and compiler, and offering a state-of-the-art macro and module system. It provides features including quote-syntax based macros, a module system similar to Racket, and an extensive standard library. The project is a Scheme compiler and runtime environment, primarily used for building high-performance applications, with a focus on ahead-of-time compilation. It is targeted towards Scheme programmers and systems developers seeking a performant and extensible Scheme implementation.
