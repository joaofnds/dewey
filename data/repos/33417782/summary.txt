`noti` is a standalone command-line utility written in Go designed to monitor processes and trigger desktop notifications upon completion. It supports notifications through various services including banner alerts, speech synthesis, and integrations with platforms like Slack, Telegram, and Pushover, offering cross-platform compatibility (macOS, Linux, and Windows). Users can easily integrate `noti` into their workflows by prepending or appending it to existing commands, or monitoring process PIDs. This developer productivity tool focuses on enhancing user experience by providing timely alerts for long-running tasks.
