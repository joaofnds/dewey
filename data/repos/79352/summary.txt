`Mail` is a Ruby library designed for comprehensive email handling, including generation, parsing, and sending. It provides functionalities for creating, reading, and manipulating email messages, supporting RFC5322 and RFC6532 standards, multipart email formats, and encodings.  The library includes wrappers for common network protocols (SMTP, POP3) and offers features like automatic encoding, attachment handling, and integration with testing frameworks. This is a Ruby library targeting developers needing to incorporate email functionalities within their applications.
