`kroki` is a JavaScript-based API and web service that generates diagrams from textual descriptions. Supporting a wide range of diagram types, including blockdiag, BPMN, C4, ERD, Mermaid, PlantUML, and UML, it converts text-based specifications into visual representations. This tool leverages various libraries and formats to render images, with a focus on developer productivity and integration, indicated by its inclusion in Hacktoberfest. It is primarily a web service and API accessible via its homepage at `https://kroki.io`.
