`org-transclusion` is an Emacs package designed to enhance note-taking and knowledge management within Org Mode by enabling transclusion. The package facilitates the inclusion of content from other Org files, improving information reuse and organization. Built using Emacs Lisp, it integrates with Org-roam, leveraging Org Mode's capabilities for structured writing and linking. This plugin/extension provides features for creating dynamic documents and managing interconnected notes, primarily targeting Emacs users who utilize Org Mode for their writing workflows.
