`mal` is a learning resource and interpreter implemented in 89 languages, designed to teach the core concepts of Lisp. It functions as a "make-a-lisp" project, with 11 incremental, testable steps demonstrating Lisp's evolution. The project includes comprehensive documentation and examples, and facilitates the creation of interpreters in diverse languages like C, Clojure, JavaScript, and WebAssembly. It presents a standalone application (CLI), facilitating the understanding of programming languages and interpreters.
