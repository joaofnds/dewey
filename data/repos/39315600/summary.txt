Iosevka is an open-source, monospace and quasi-proportional typeface family designed for code, terminals, and technical documents.  Built primarily with JavaScript, it provides numerous weights, widths, and slopes across sans-serif and slab-serif subfamilies. Key features include extensive character support, including Latin, Greek, Cyrillic, and IPA symbols, alongside stylistic sets, character variants, and ligatures, including language-specific ligatures. This is a font project, delivering distributable font files and offering a custom build process for user-specific configurations, primarily targeting developers and technical writers.
