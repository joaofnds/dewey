`notespace` is a Clojure library providing a notebook-like experience within a Clojure development environment, aiming to integrate well with existing literate programming tools. It enables users to create and interact with code examples within their Clojure namespaces, facilitating code sharing and documentation. The project leverages the Clojure language and is related to the broader ecosystem of Clojure visual tools, including projects like Clay and Kindly. `notespace` is primarily a developer productivity tool, focusing on streamlining the workflow for Clojure developers.
