`avelino/awesome-go` is a curated, community-driven list of Go frameworks, libraries, and software, serving as a comprehensive resource for Go developers. This project functions as a categorized index, linking to diverse Go projects within various domains, including web development, data structures, databases, testing, and more. It leverages Go's ecosystem extensively, covering topics like Go's standard library, popular third-party libraries, and practical examples, functioning as an educational resource. This is a reference/index project, with its primary audience being Go developers of all levels.
