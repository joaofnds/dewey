`tb-keycast` is an Emacs Lisp minor mode providing real-time display of pressed keys and corresponding function names within the Emacs tab bar. It enhances developer productivity by showing keybindings as they are used, including a repeat counter, with alignment options for left or right side of the tab bar. This plugin for Emacs requires version 29.1 or later, utilizing Emacs Lisp as its core technology. It is intended as an Emacs extension, specifically targeting Emacs users to improve their workflow.
