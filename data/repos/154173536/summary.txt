Planify is a standalone task management application designed for the GNOME desktop environment, providing Todoist and Nextcloud synchronization. Written in Vala and utilizing the Libadwaita library, it features a drag-and-order interface, progress indicators for projects, reminder notifications, and night mode support. Its core functionality centers on organizing tasks, sections, and events, offering offline Todoist support and quick find capabilities. The project is built using Meson and depends on libraries like GTK4, Libsoup, and WebKitGTK. It's intended for end users who need to manage their tasks and synchronize them with Todoist and Nextcloud.
