Waypoint is a Go-based developer tool designed to automate the build, deployment, and release of applications across various platforms. It supports multiple build methods, including Cloud Native Buildpacks and Docker, and targets deployment to Kubernetes, AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, among others. Functionality includes defining application lifecycles as code, streamlining workflows for developers. As a standalone CLI application, Waypoint aims to enhance developer productivity by providing a consistent and repeatable deployment process.
