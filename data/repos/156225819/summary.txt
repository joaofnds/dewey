`hl-fill-column` is an Emacs Lisp package designed to highlight the fill column, a text editing guideline. Its primary functionality is to visually emphasize the fill column within an Emacs editing buffer, aiding in text formatting and readability. This is a plugin/extension specifically for the Emacs text editor, utilizing the Emacs Lisp programming language to provide this functionality. The target audience is users of the Emacs text editor who desire improved visual feedback for text wrapping and line length adherence.
