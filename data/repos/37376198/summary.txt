`TestDisk & PhotoRec` is a standalone application written in C, providing data recovery utilities. TestDisk recovers lost partitions and supports a wide array of filesystems, including FAT, NTFS, Ext2/3, HFS+, and others. PhotoRec, a companion tool, recovers files from various storage media, supporting over 480 file extensions. Targeting system administrators and users needing data recovery, the software runs on multiple operating systems including Windows, Linux, and BSD variants.
