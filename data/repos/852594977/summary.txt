**pg_mooncake** is a Postgres extension that enhances data warehousing performance by leveraging columnar storage and vectorized execution using DuckDB. It enables fast analytics within Postgres, ranking among the top 10 fastest in ClickBench. The extension stores columnstore tables as Iceberg or Delta Lake tables in local file system or cloud storage, offering compatibility with popular data lakehouse architectures like Parquet and delta-lake. Maintained by Mooncake Labs, pg_mooncake is available on Neon Postgres and supports transactions, updates, deletes, joins, and more while maintaining the speed and efficiency of columnar storage. This library targets data engineers, analysts, and developers working with large-scale data warehousing and analytics projects.
