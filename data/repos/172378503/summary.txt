Powerlevel10k is a highly customizable Zsh theme designed for speed, flexibility, and a superior out-of-the-box experience. It provides a configuration wizard for easy setup, along with features like instant prompts, transient prompts, and extensive customization options for its appearance. Utilizing the Shell scripting language, Powerlevel10k displays various prompt segments displaying information from a wide array of sources and offers compatibility with Powerlevel9k and Pure themes. This is a plugin/theme designed to enhance the user experience within the Zsh shell, primarily targeting developers and power users who customize their terminal environments.
