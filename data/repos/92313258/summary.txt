cert-manager is a Kubernetes add-on automating the provisioning and management of TLS certificates. Written in Go, it introduces custom resource definitions (CRDs) to Kubernetes for defining and managing certificates and certificate issuers. Key functionalities include obtaining certificates from sources like Let's Encrypt and HashiCorp Vault, automated certificate renewal, and integration with ingress controllers. This project is a standalone application and targets system administrators and Kubernetes developers seeking to secure their cluster deployments.
