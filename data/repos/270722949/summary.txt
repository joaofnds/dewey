Lucide is an open-source icon library written in TypeScript, providing a collection of over 1000 vector (SVG) files for incorporating icons into digital and non-digital projects. The library offers several official packages, including `lucide`, `lucide-react`, `lucide-vue-next`, `lucide-svelte`, and `lucide-solid`, each designed to facilitate easy integration with popular front-end frameworks. Lucide aims to simplify the process of using icons in projects, catering to both designers and developers. This library is particularly useful for those seeking a consistent icon toolkit that can be easily integrated into various digital interfaces.
