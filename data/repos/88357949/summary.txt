`i3status-rust` is a standalone application designed to replace the i3status utility, providing a customizable status bar for window managers like i3 and sway. Written in Rust, it parses the i3bar protocol to display system information blocks such as time, battery status, and volume, offering a resource-friendly and feature-rich alternative. Key functionalities include theming and icon support, configuration via TOML files, and the ability to trigger block updates via signals. It targets users of i3 and sway window managers and developers seeking a customizable system status display.
