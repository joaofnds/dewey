The `SanFranciscoFont` repository provides the San Francisco font family, developed by Apple, for use in digital design projects. It serves as a resource for incorporating Apple's proprietary typeface into applications, specifically targeting iOS, macOS, and watchOS platforms. Primarily a font resource, the repository allows developers to access and integrate the San Francisco font into their projects. The project's functionality directly supports user interface design and development, enhancing the visual presentation across various Apple devices.
