`Command-T` is a Lua-based plugin designed for Neovim and Vim, providing rapid "fuzzy" file navigation. It enables users to swiftly open files, buffers, tags, help, and execute commands via a minimal keystroke interface, employing a hand-crafted C-based matching algorithm and parallelized search for optimal speed. The plugin incorporates advanced features, including path-aware character weighting and integration with Watchman, and is configurable for opening files in various window layouts. As a Vim/Neovim plugin, its primary users are developers who use these editors.
