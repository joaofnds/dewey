`procs` is a Rust-based, command-line utility designed as a modern replacement for the `ps` command, offering enhanced process monitoring capabilities. Key features include colored and human-readable output, multi-column keyword search, support for various process information such as TCP/UDP ports and Docker container names, and a tree view.  It provides pager support, a watch mode for real-time updates, and integrates with multiple package managers and operating systems including Linux, macOS, Windows, and FreeBSD, offering a wide variety of installation methods, and targeting system administrators and developers who require process management tools.
