`org-tufte` is an Emacs Lisp package designed to convert Org-mode files into HTML documents styled with Tufte-CSS. It provides a streamlined workflow for generating visually appealing, information-rich documents suitable for academic or presentation purposes. The core functionality centers around exporting Org-mode syntax, leveraging `orgmode` and `tufte-css` technologies to produce the desired output. This project functions as an Emacs package, specifically catering to users of Emacs and Org-mode who wish to create elegant, well-formatted documents directly from their plain text files.
