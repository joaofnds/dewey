`type-challenges/type-challenges` is a collection of TypeScript type challenges with an online judge, serving as a developer productivity tool and educational resource. It provides a platform for users to enhance their understanding of TypeScript's type system and practice writing advanced type utilities. The project features a wide array of challenges categorized by difficulty, from warm-up exercises to extreme-level problems. It leverages TypeScript as its core language, offering an environment for developers to explore and refine their type-level programming skills.
