`xsv` is a fast command-line toolkit written in Rust, designed for efficient manipulation and analysis of CSV files. Key functionalities include indexing, slicing, joining, sorting, and statistical analysis of CSV data, along with options for reformatting and data transformation. The project is implemented as a standalone CLI application and leverages Rust's performance characteristics to offer fast processing speeds, particularly when handling large datasets. Its primary audience is likely data engineers and analysts who work with CSV data and require a performant, flexible command-line tool for data exploration and preparation.
