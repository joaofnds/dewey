Netron is a standalone, JavaScript-based visualizer application for neural network, deep learning, and machine learning models. It supports a wide range of model formats, including ONNX, TensorFlow Lite, Core ML, Keras, and PyTorch, enabling users to inspect and understand model architectures. Users can open model files locally or access them via URL, providing a user-friendly interface to visualize model graphs and layers. This application primarily caters to machine learning engineers and researchers working with diverse model types and frameworks.
