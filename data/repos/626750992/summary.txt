`failsafe-go/failsafe-go` is a Go library designed to implement fault tolerance and resilience patterns in Go applications. It provides a suite of resilience policies, including retry, circuit breaker, rate limiter, timeout, fallback, hedge, bulkhead, and caching, for wrapping functions to enhance their robustness. This library empowers developers to build more reliable and resilient systems by offering composable and configurable fault handling mechanisms. It is a developer productivity tool, a library focused on enhancing the reliability of Go applications.
