`youtube-dl` is a command-line application written in Python designed for downloading videos from YouTube.com and numerous other video platforms. Its core functionality includes downloading videos from a wide variety of sources, supporting format selection, playlist and subtitle downloads, and audio extraction.  The application provides extensive options for customization, including proxy settings, download rate limiting, file naming templates, and post-processing with external tools like FFmpeg.  It's a standalone, cross-platform application, and its target audience appears to be technically-minded users who want to download and manage video content from various online sources.
