`base16-i3status-rust` provides base16 color scheme templates for theming the i3status-rust status bar application. It offers configuration snippets derived from the base16 color palette, specifically optimized for dark themes, based on the Solarized Dark built-in theme of i3status-rust. The repository acts as a template resource for developers customizing the appearance of their i3status-rust configurations, streamlining the application of base16 color schemes. It is a configuration template primarily intended for system administrators or users who customize their i3 window manager environment.
