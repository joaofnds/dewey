`org-modern` is an Emacs Lisp project focused on providing a modern style for Org-mode, a plain-text markup system for note-taking, project planning, and authoring within the Emacs text editor. Its core functionality involves customizing the appearance and styling of Org-mode documents within Emacs, enhancing the user experience and visual appeal. It is a plugin or customization for the Emacs text editor, written in Emacs Lisp.
