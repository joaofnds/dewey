Nylas Mail is an open-source, extensible desktop email client built using JavaScript, Electron, React, and Flux.  It provides email management and productivity features, with the ability to extend functionality through third-party plugins and themes. The application leverages a modular architecture, with separate packages for isomorphic core components, client-side application logic, syncing, and cloud-based services. This standalone GUI application targeted both individual users and developers, and supported multiple operating systems including macOS, Windows, and Linux.
