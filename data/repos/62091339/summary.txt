kOps is a standalone command-line application written in Go for automating the installation, upgrades, and management of production-grade Kubernetes clusters. It provisions the necessary cloud infrastructure on supported platforms, including AWS, GCP, DigitalOcean, Hetzner, OpenStack, and Azure. Functionality includes cluster creation, destruction, and maintenance, operating similarly to `kubectl` for cluster operations. This developer productivity tool targets system administrators and DevOps engineers managing Kubernetes deployments.
