`arrow-py/arrow` is a Python library designed to provide a more intuitive and user-friendly approach to handling dates and times. It simplifies date and time manipulations, including timezone conversions, formatting, and parsing, offering a higher-level abstraction than Python's built-in `datetime` module.  The library's core functionality centers around making date and time operations easier for developers, as evidenced by its descriptive keywords and homepage documentation.  It is a developer productivity tool focusing on the Python ecosystem, addressing a core need for improved date and time management within Python applications.
