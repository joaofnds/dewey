Background Music is a standalone macOS audio utility designed to enhance audio management. It provides features including automatic music pausing based on other audio activity, per-application volume controls, and system audio recording capabilities. The application is written in C++ and leverages Objective-C for macOS-specific functionality, with keywords indicating the use of audio APIs. This project is a GUI application targeting macOS users who desire advanced control over their system audio, including recording and individual application volume adjustments.
