Oban is an Elixir library providing robust background job processing, designed for reliability and observability, with built-in support for PostgreSQL, MySQL, and SQLite3 databases. Its core functionality centers around managing and executing jobs within isolated queues, offering features like transactional control, job canceling, scheduled and periodic job execution, priority management, and historic metrics tracking for analysis. This library enables developers to offload asynchronous tasks and ensures job data persistence, crucial for production environments; Oban offers advanced features through its commercial "Pro" extension pack.
