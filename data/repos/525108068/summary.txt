Mona Sans is a variable font repository providing a versatile typeface designed for use across web, product, and print applications. It offers a variable font implementation, enabling granular control over weight, width, and slant through CSS `@font-face` rules, along with preloading optimization for reduced Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS). The project includes extensive stylistic sets and ligatures, facilitating design flexibility and customization. It is a font library, licensed under the SIL Open Font License v1.1, targeted towards frontend developers and designers seeking adaptable typographic solutions.
